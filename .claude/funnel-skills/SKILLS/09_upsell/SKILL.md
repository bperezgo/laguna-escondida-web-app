## Step 2 — Output structure

**1. Post-purchase onboarding sequence (first 72 hours)**

Email 1 — Immediate: confirmation + access + quick win
Email 2 — Day 1: how to start + what to expect
Email 3 — Day 3: check-in + first milestone

**2. Upsell opportunities map**

For each customer journey moment, identify:
- What the customer needs next
- What additional product/service solves that need
- When and how to present it (timing and format)
- Upsell offer copy

**3. Downsell (if the customer doesn't upgrade)**

Lower-price or lower-commitment alternative to avoid losing the lead:
- What to offer
- When to activate it
- Downsell email/page copy

**4. Referral program**

Structure the program:
- Mechanics (what incentive, how it works)
- Email to activate referrals from satisfied customers
- Share message template (what the customer sends to contacts)

**5. Reactivation sequence**

For customers who purchased but have not engaged:
- Trigger: when it activates (X days of inactivity)
- 3 reactivation emails
