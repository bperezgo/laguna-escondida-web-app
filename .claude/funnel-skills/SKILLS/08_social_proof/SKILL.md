# Skill: Social Proof and Credibility (08_social_proof)

Structures all social evidence in the funnel to maximize visitor trust.

## Step 0 — Output language

Ask the user what language they want the output written in. Default: **Spanish**.

> "What language should I use for the generated content? (default: Spanish)"

Apply the chosen language to all output text for this skill.

## Step 2 — Social proof inventory

First, ask the user (or extract from context) what social proof currently exists:
- Text testimonials
- Video testimonials
- Results screenshots
- Aggregate data (number of clients, success rate)
- Media mentions, podcasts, publications
- Certifications or third-party endorsements

If no social proof exists yet, generate a guide on how to collect it.

## Step 3 — Output structure

**1. Optimized testimonials**

For each testimonial received, rewrite it in the maximum-impact format:
```
Before: [specific situation before buying]
After: [concrete and measurable result]
Direct quote: "[exact phrase in the client's own voice]"
— [Full Name], [occupation/city]
```

**2. Long case study (1–2 cases)**

Complete narrative structure:
- Who the client was before (situation, problem)
- What led them to buy
- How they used the product
- Concrete result with numbers
- What changed in their life

**3. Credibility data**

Generate credibility microcopy for use on the landing:
- "More than [X] [clients/students/companies]"
- "[X]% of [result metric]"
- "Since [year] helping..."

**4. Logos / mentions section**

If applicable, structure the "As seen in" or "Trusted by companies like" section.

**5. Guide to collect testimonials**

If the business has few testimonials, generate:
- Email template to request testimonials from current clients
- 5 guiding questions to get high-quality testimonials
- Case study form template
